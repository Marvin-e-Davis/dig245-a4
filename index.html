CONDUCTED BY: VIRGINIA, LAUREN, MARVIN, AND JORDAN

Test Overview

This test was conducted to examine the usabilty of Yale School of the Art's website. A series of tasks were executed by our tester Jordan Reed who then gave feedback on his experience navigating the website.

User Quote

“Is this a trick? Does it even exist? This website is efficient at making me want to leave!” - Tester Jordan Reed

Conclusion

The Yale Schol of Art Website is a creative but chaotic website that is troublesome to naviagte and maneuver even when trying to complete the most simplest of tasks. The website’s contant changes make users unfamiliar with the environment feel as if they’re visiting a new website every time. The lack of clarity when attempting to find resources or infromation leads to a frustration that makes you wish you didn’t have to use the website all together. The website sacrifices clarity and usability for bits of creativity and a fluid environement. In the end this exchange isn’t most desirable for users.
